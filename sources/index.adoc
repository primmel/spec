= Primmel specification
:copyright-year: 2026
:language: en
:mn-document-class: ribose
:local-cache-only:

Primmel is the public successor to MMEL (Multi-Modal Modelling Language), a
domain-specific modelling language developed jointly by Ribose and BSI for the
BSI SMART programme and now adopted as the basis of OIML SMART for legal
metrology certification.

Primmel unifies data modelling, process modelling, compliance tracking,
quantitative measurement, and cross-model mapping into a single executable
notation. The same model serves as a referenceable standard, an executable
programme, and an audit target.
